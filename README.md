# Banking Fraud Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents a Banking Fraud Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze customer risk behavior, monitor fraud trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard provides insights into fraud rates, high-risk customers, credit score performance, debt exposure, and customer segmentation through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

## Key Metrics

* Total Customers: 13K
* Total Applications: 100K
* Fraud Rate: 0.54
* Average Annual Income: 50.51K
* Average Outstanding Debt: 1.43K
* High Risk Customers: 54K

## Features

* Fraud Risk Monitoring
* Customer Risk Segmentation
* Credit Score Analysis
* EMI Delay Analysis
* Occupation-wise Risk Analysis
* Age Group-wise Risk Analysis
* Monthly High-Risk Trend Tracking
* Interactive Slicers and Filters

## Dashboard Insights

* Customers aged 18–50 contribute the highest risk volume.
* Poor credit scores are associated with higher payment delays.
* Higher outstanding debt correlates with increased fraud risk.
* Occupation-based analysis helps identify vulnerable customer segments.
* Monthly trend analysis enables proactive fraud monitoring.
